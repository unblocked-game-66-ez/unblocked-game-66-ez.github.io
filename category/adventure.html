Adventure games are a genre that emphasizes exploration, storytelling, and puzzle-solving, immersing players in richly crafted narratives where they interact with characters, environments, and objects to progress. Typically, players embark on quests, solve challenges, and unravel mysteries, often requiring them to gather items, unlock secrets, and make decisions that influence the storyline. These games are generally more focused on narrative and discovery than action or combat, offering a slower-paced, reflective experience. Titles like The Legend of Zelda and Monkey Island are iconic in the genre, blending intriguing worlds, deep storytelling, and thought-provoking puzzles to engage players on both intellectual and emotional levels.
