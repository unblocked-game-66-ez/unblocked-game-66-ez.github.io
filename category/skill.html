Skill games are a genre of video games that emphasize player dexterity, timing, and strategic thinking to achieve success, often relying on the players physical and mental abilities rather than chance. These games can include a wide range of activities, such as platformers like Super Mario Bros., where precise jumps and timing are crucial, or puzzle games like Tetris, which require quick thinking and spatial awareness. Players are challenged to improve their skills through practice, with many skill games featuring increasing levels of difficulty that test their abilities as they progress. This genre appeals to a wide audience, as it fosters a sense of accomplishment through mastery and encourages competition among players aiming for high scores or faster completion times.
