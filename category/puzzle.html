Puzzle games are a genre of video games that challenge players to solve logic-based problems, riddles, or patterns, often requiring critical thinking, strategy, and creativity. These games can range from simple matching mechanics, like Candy Crush, where players align objects to clear levels, to more complex brain teasers, such as Portal or The Witness, which demand spatial reasoning and problem-solving skills. Puzzle games typically feature levels that increase in difficulty as players progress, providing a satisfying blend of mental stimulation and reward. They appeal to a broad audience due to their accessibility, variety, and ability to engage the mind, offering both casual play and more challenging experiences.
